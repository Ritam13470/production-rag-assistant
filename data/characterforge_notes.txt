CharacterForge AI is a browser-based 3D character creation platform.

The project allows users to upload 3D models, preview them, customize colors, save uploaded models, and reopen them from the dashboard.

The backend uses Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Cloudinary for persistent model uploads.

The frontend is inspired by Sketchfab-style 3D model pages with model viewer, download actions, share actions, embed actions, and polished asset presentation.

The main goal of CharacterForge AI is to help creators build, customize, manage, and present 3D characters in a premium web interface.
